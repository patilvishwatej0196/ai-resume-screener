Job Title: Data Analyst
Company: Insight Analytics India
Location: Bangalore, Karnataka
Experience Required: 0-2 years (Freshers welcome)

About the Role:
We are hiring a Data Analyst to help us turn raw business data into
meaningful insights. You will work with sales, marketing, and product teams.

Required Skills:
- Python or R for data analysis
- pandas and numpy for data manipulation
- SQL for querying databases
- Data visualization using matplotlib, seaborn, or Power BI
- Excel and Google Sheets proficiency
- Basic statistics and probability

Good to Have:
- Experience with Tableau or Looker
- Knowledge of machine learning basics
- Familiarity with Jupyter Notebook

Responsibilities:
- Collect, clean, and analyze large datasets
- Create dashboards and visual reports
- Identify trends and patterns in business data
- Present findings to non-technical stakeholders
- Automate repetitive reporting tasks using Python
- Support data-driven decision making

Qualification: B.E. / B.Tech / B.Sc in Computer Science, Statistics, or Mathematics
